Brainy Bunny - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Introduction

Brainy Bunny ("we", "our", or "the app") is a brain training puzzle game designed for children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Brainy Bunny does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, including but not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Photos, videos, or audio

Local Data Storage

The app stores game progress (such as completed levels and star ratings) and user preferences (such as sound settings) locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any server and is only accessible by the app itself.

Internet Access

Brainy Bunny requires internet access for optional features such as rewarded advertisements (e.g., to earn extra hints). No personal data is collected or transmitted by the app itself.

Third-Party Services

Brainy Bunny may use third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob) to display optional rewarded advertisements. These services may collect anonymous device information and ad interaction data in accordance with their own privacy policies. No personally identifiable information is shared with these services by the app.

For more information about how Google uses data, visit: Google Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Brainy Bunny is designed to be safe for children of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. Since no data is collected, there is no data to share, sell, or disclose to third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
